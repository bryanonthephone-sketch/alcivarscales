FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Still on the fence? We've answered the most common questions right here.

What exactly is Local SEO?
Local SEO is the process of optimizing your business to appear at the top of Google Maps and local search results when people in your area search for your services. It's the most cost-effective way to attract local customers without paying per click.
How fast will I see results?
Most clients see measurable improvement within 30–45 days. Our guarantee is Top 3 within 90 days on the Standard plan — and if we don't deliver, you don't pay. Simple as that.
Do I need a website?
Not necessarily. While a website helps, we can often achieve top 3 rankings using just your Google Business Profile. We'll tell you during the free analysis whether a site is needed for your specific market.
What's the money-back guarantee?
On the Standard and Quarterly plans, if you're not in the Top 3 within 90 days, we keep working for free until you are — or we refund your last month. No fine print, no loopholes.
How is this different from Google Ads?
With Google Ads, you pay for every click and the traffic stops when you stop paying. Local SEO builds organic rankings that last years with no per-click costs. Most clients see 5–10x better ROI vs ads.
What industries do you work with?
Any local service business — contractors, plumbers, lawyers, dentists, restaurants, gyms, salons, and more. If your customers search locally on Google, we can get you to the top 3.
